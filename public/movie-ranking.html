The Best Movies According to Josh

My top 5 movies of all-time, currently.

The Fountain

I love the the ideas in this movie and this music is some of the best from Clint Mansell.

500 Days of Summer

This movie just speaks to me and how I felt my life went for a while. Always believing in true love or "the one". Joseph Gordon Levitt is one of my favorite actors and he shines in this movie.

Green Street Hooligans

Elijah Wood is very believeable in the role of an American getting involved in soccer (futbol) hooligans over the pond. Charlie Hunnam is so cool.

Interstellar

The experience of this movie in the AMC IMAX is one that I will never forget. The sound and music is phenomenal. The visuals are awesome. The casting is perfect. And I love the story. It is just one of those movies that will always be at the top of my list.

Scream

It is just a classic movie and I love the whole series. I felt like it needed to be on here, representing the horror genre.
